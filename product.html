Made of two honest materials

WICK starts as a cylinder of solid wood — ash with its pale, calm grain, or walnut with its deep coffee tones. The lower body is finished in a soft matte white that meets the wood in a clean, precise line. Behind it, a round wooden disc anchors the piece to your wall and hides the fixing completely.

The steel tip at the front is the working end: rounded so it never snags a sweater or scratches a bag, solid so it never bends. Hang a heavy winter coat, a wet towel, a full tote — WICK doesn't care. It just holds, quietly, for years.
